About Us

Together, we are on a mission to deliver the finest pizza in town from our mobile oven to your street, event, or home. With locally sourced ingredients, a roaring hot oven, and a whole lot of heart, every pizza we make is crafted to be remembered.

Our Story

Finest Pizza in Town

Founded in 2025 by Andrei and Alexandra.
Our pizza venture is all about passion, precision, and bringing people together through unforgettable food. Andrei, head chef, brings years of experience and a deep love for traditional wood-fired pizza. His dedication to artisanal techniques results in each slice being filled with vibrant, rustic flavours that are both authentic and unforgettable. Alexandra keeps everything running smoothly. From event coordination to customer service, she makes sure every interaction is as memorable as our food.
